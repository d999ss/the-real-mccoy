# GRAMA Request — Park City Municipal Corp

**To:** records@parkcity.org
**Cc:** Park City Records Officer
**Subject:** GRAMA Request — Insight Public Sector and CDW purchase orders, FY2022–FY2026
**Date:** 2026-04-25

---

Pursuant to the Utah Government Records Access and Management Act, Utah Code § 63G-2, I request the following records held by Park City Municipal Corporation:

## Records requested

1. **All purchase orders** issued by Park City Municipal Corporation to **Insight Public Sector, Inc.** (and any related Insight Direct USA Inc., Insight Public Sector Inc., or Insight LLC entities) covering the period **July 1, 2021 through the date of this request**. Please include each purchase order in its native form with all line-item product descriptions, SKUs, quantities, unit prices, and total amounts.

2. **All invoices** received by Park City Municipal Corporation from Insight Public Sector or related Insight entities for the same period, in native form.

3. **All purchase orders and invoices** between Park City Municipal Corporation and **CDW Government, Inc.** (also doing business as CDW Government LLC, CDW LLC, or CDW-G) covering the period **July 1, 2021 through the date of this request**, in the same level of detail.

4. **All contracts, master service agreements, statements of work, and amendments** between Park City Municipal Corporation and Flock Safety, Inc. (also d/b/a Flock Group, Inc.) for any period.

5. **All records evidencing Park City Municipal Corporation's purchase, lease, subscription, or use of Automated License Plate Reader (ALPR) hardware, software, or services**, including but not limited to:
   - Cooperative purchasing contracts under OMNIA Partners contract R250203
   - User account creation records on the Flock Safety platform
   - Records of personnel granted query access to any ALPR network, including the Flock Nationwide Lookup network
   - Any data-sharing agreements with other agencies relating to ALPR data

6. **The currently effective ALPR usage policy** required to be published under Utah Code § 41-6a-2003(2)(a). If no such policy has been adopted or published, please so state in writing.

7. **The annual ALPR report** filed (or required to be filed) with the Utah Commission on Criminal and Juvenile Justice for the March 1, 2026 deadline under Utah Code § 41-6a-2004.

## Format

I request that responsive records be provided in native electronic format where possible (PDF, CSV, XLSX). I do not require certified or paper copies.

## Fees

I am willing to pay reasonable fees up to **$50** for the production of these records. Please notify me in writing before incurring fees that would exceed this amount, and provide an itemized estimate at that time.

## Public-interest fee waiver

I request a fee waiver under Utah Code § 63G-2-203(4)(b) on the basis that release of these records is in the public interest. The records concern the deployment, funding, and oversight of license plate reader surveillance technology operated within or affecting Park City — a matter of substantial and demonstrable public interest, and one for which no comparable disclosure currently exists in any public dataset.

## Statutory exemptions

I acknowledge that some records may be subject to statutory protection under Utah Code § 63G-2-302 through § 63G-2-305. Where redactions are applied, I request that:

- Each redaction be accompanied by a citation to the specific subsection relied upon
- A redacted version of each record be provided alongside any withheld portion
- Records be released in part where any non-protected portion is reasonably segregable

## Response timeline

Please acknowledge receipt of this request within five business days as required by Utah Code § 63G-2-204(3)(a), and provide a substantive response within ten business days as required by § 63G-2-204(3)(b), or in the case of an extraordinary circumstance, provide written notice of any extension.
