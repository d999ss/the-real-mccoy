## GRAMA #2 — UDOT Special Use Permits

**To:** records@utah.gov · **Cc:** rights-of-way@utah.gov · **Re:** UDOT Right-of-Way records

Pursuant to Utah Code § 63G-2, I request the following records held by the Utah Department of Transportation:

1. The complete list of all currently active **Automatic License Plate Reader System (ALPRS) Special Use Permits** issued under Utah Admin. Code R920-4-16 for installations within Summit County and Wasatch County, Utah, from January 1, 2020 to the date of this request. For each permit, please include: applicant agency, permit issuance date, permit expiration date (if applicable), GPS coordinates of every camera authorized under the permit, the route(s) on which cameras are installed, the contractor performing installation, and the URL of the agency's published policy as required by Utah Code § 41-6a-2003(2)(a).

2. All **denials, withdrawals, and revocations** of ALPRS Special Use Permits within the same geographic and temporal scope, with reasons.

3. All **License Agreements** between UDOT and Flock Group Inc, Flock Safety, Motorola Solutions, Vigilant Solutions LLC, or any other ALPR contractor, currently in effect or terminated since January 1, 2020.

4. All **encroachment permits** issued for ALPR-related installation work within Summit and Wasatch counties from January 1, 2020 to the present, including those filed under utility, fiber, conduit, or general infrastructure descriptions where the underlying work supported ALPR equipment.

5. All correspondence sent to or received from `row@utah.gov` regarding ALPRS Special Use Permits in Summit or Wasatch counties since January 1, 2024.

Format: native electronic format (PDF, CSV, XLSX). Public-interest fee waiver requested under Utah Code § 63G-2-203(4)(b).
